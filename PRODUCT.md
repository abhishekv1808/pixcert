# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Startup founders and small-business owners in Bangalore (and wider India) shopping for an agency to build their website, brand identity, or marketing. They arrive skeptical, compare 3–4 agencies, and decide based on whether the agency's own site proves the craft it sells. Primary conversion: quote form, WhatsApp, or a Calendly discovery call.

## Product Purpose

ITBIZONE's marketing site. Every page is a portfolio piece in disguise: the site itself is the strongest proof that ITBIZONE can build fast, polished, modern web experiences. Success = enquiries (quote form submissions, WhatsApp messages, booked calls).

## Brand Personality

Bold, cinematic, precise. Confident enough to use giant type and dark panels; disciplined enough to keep every animation purposeful. Speaks plainly to business owners, not developers.

## Anti-references

- Generic template agency sites (stock hero + three feature cards + testimonial slider).
- Corporate IT-services sites with blue gradients and handshake photos.
- Overloaded animation showcases where motion hides thin content.

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach** — the site demonstrates the web craft being sold; every section is a proof point.
2. **Dark/cream rhythm** — sections alternate `#0a0a0a` dark panels and `#f5f2ec` cream; no two dark sections touch.
3. **Motion is depth, not decoration** — scroll-linked parallax and 3D create spatial hierarchy; entrances run once; everything respects `prefers-reduced-motion`.
4. **The conversion path is always one glance away** — quote CTA, WhatsApp, floating CTA persist through every fold.
5. **One accent** — primary orange `#ff4a17` carries all emphasis; headings in Bricolage Grotesque, body in Inter.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

`prefers-reduced-motion` honored in every animated component (static fallbacks, not missing content). Focus-visible outlines, aria labels on icon links, decorative elements `aria-hidden`. Target WCAG AA contrast on text.
